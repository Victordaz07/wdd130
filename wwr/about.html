Dry Oar White Water Rafting

At Dry Oar, we create unforgettable rafting adventures where families and friends reconnect with nature and each other in a safe and thrilling way.

History

Dry Oar was founded by outdoor enthusiasts who loved sharing the river with others. Over the years we have become a trusted rafting company with a passion for adventure.

Our experienced guides, careful planning, and focus on safety allow each guest to enjoy the excitement of the rapids while feeling confident on the water.
