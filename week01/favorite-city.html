My Favorite City

Santo Domingo, Dominican Republic

Santo Domingo is my favorite city because it feels both historic and alive. It is the capital of the Dominican Republic and the heart of my country culture. I love walking through the Colonial Zone and visiting the Santo Domingo Temple, which reminds me of peace and faith in the middle of a busy city. The people, the food, and the ocean breeze make this city truly special to me.

Victor Ruiz Bello
